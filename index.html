Support Operations & Systems Specialist — Bridging Engineering & Customer Experience

I’m a trilingual support operations and systems specialist focused on removing friction so teams and users can do their best work. Currently at Logitech, I drive digital transformation for Logitech for Business (L4B) by optimizing support tool architecture and leading AI-driven workflow implementations.

My approach to support architecture combines a deep technical curiosity with an empathetic, human-centric focus. I look at complex ecosystems—whether it's automating workflows in Zendesk or troubleshooting hardware—and find ways to make technology respect people, support them, and get out of their way.

My path to tech operations wasn't linear, and that’s my superpower:

  • Live Production & Theatre: Spent years handling A/V setups and tech support for live events. It taught me to troubleshoot under immense pressure, communicate clearly, and stay composed when timing is everything.
  • Fairphone: Deepened my technical support roots by resolving complex Tier 1/2 hardware and software cases, investigating root causes, and training teams.
  • Full-Stack Development: Graduated from a full-stack bootcamp (Ruby, JavaScript, HTML/CSS, PostgreSQL), giving me the technical vocabulary to bridge the gap between customer-facing teams and engineering.

I thrive at the intersection of human connection, creative problem-solving, and robust technical infrastructure. If that resonates, let’s connect.
